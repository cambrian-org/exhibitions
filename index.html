First Signs of Vision

Journey through the evolution of sight across 540 million years

Experience the Dawn of Sight

First Signs of Vision is an interactive exhibit that lets you interact directly with evolution. Experience the evolutionary history of vision from the first light-sensitive cells to modern vision. Start your journey 540 million years ago as a bacteria and choose your own evolutionary path to vision found in present day animals.
